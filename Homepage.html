About

Welcome to St.Thomas Hospital. We are grateful you found us. We have a mission to serve and provide affordable and quality medical services to the general public. We were founded in 2001 by the People Ready to Serve Foundation and have served our mission ever since. We have well-trained doctors who provide quality healthcare.
